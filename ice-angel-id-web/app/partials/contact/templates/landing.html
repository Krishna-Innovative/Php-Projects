The #TWOL campaign emerges from the actuality that we’re all facing the same storm, but not everyone is in the same boat. We all have different circumstances, and yet we all have something to offer. No one is powerless, and no one is useless. The #TWOL campaign is absolutely not about charity and has everything to do with effecting change – starting with you.

The heart of the #TWOL campaign is that we’re giving away 100% of your premium subscription revenue over the next three months ending 31 July 2020 to several amazing grassroots organizations that we’ve partnered with to leverage and transform these funds into lifegiving measures in their local communities. We’re creating a value chain to support the millions of additional lives around the world that are being threatened as a direct result of lockdown measures that are starving daily wage workers and their families of food and other critical resources like medication.
